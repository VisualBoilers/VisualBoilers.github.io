In this homework, we decided to explore some interesting things about adopted movies. We made the following visualizations using Tableau software:

  • Adopted movies. This table represents how many adopted movies were produced every year and how many movies were produced per author at that year. As can be seen from this table, the total number of adopted movies over designated period reveals a general trend of fluctuations.
  • Comparision between movie names & book names. In this chart, the number of different names and the number of same names between book and movies are visualized. As can be seen from this table, most movies kept the its book's name.
  • Popular authors. We encode the number of adopted movies of the author by bubble size. Stephen King is absolutely the most popular author.
